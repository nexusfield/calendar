## What I'm Building Outside of AIL

**NEXUS** — a personal intelligence system. Local-first, transitioning to cloud-hosted.

Core stack: Next.js, Supabase with pgvector, Claude APIs, Vercel.

**The insight that drove it:** AI has a critical gap — it can't store and compound tacit knowledge. Every conversation resets. NEXUS solves this with a RAG memory architecture. The quality of what gets stored matters more than the retrieval mechanism — deliberately chosen, evolving knowledge, not scraped surface-level data.

**Current honest assessment:** Retrieval works. But the conversation doesn't feel totally there. It feels like a smart AI with notes rather than something that genuinely *thinks with* you. That gap may be harder than the original build.

**Nexus 3.0 vision — Behavioral Mirror System:**
A pipeline capturing the gap between who you intend to be each day and who you actually are.

- Morning intention snapshot
- Real-time updates throughout the day
- Evening debrief — plan vs. execution
- Behavioral model builds over time

Interface stack: OpenClaw (Telegram/SMS/voice, always-on), Field Book (calendar layer), NEXUS (memory layer).

**Infrastructure:** Old desktop PC converted to personal server — i7, 32GB RAM, RTX 3060, Ubuntu Server, Cloudflare Tunnel, Ollama running Qwen 14B locally.

**Underlying principle:** Full data sovereignty. Life does not run on someone else's servers. Own the hardware, own the data, own the intelligence.
